Our Chai Story

The Origins

Founded in 1990, Chai-Wala began as a humble street-side tea stall with a vision to serve the perfect cup of chai. Our journey started when our founder, inspired by the rich tradition of Indian tea culture, decided to bring authentic chai experiences to modern tea lovers.

The Art of Brewing

We source our tea leaves from the finest estates in Assam and Darjeeling. Each cup is crafted with precision, following age-old recipes that have been passed down through generations. Our signature masala chai blend combines premium black tea with hand-ground spices.

Growing Together

From a single stall to multiple locations, our growth has been fueled by the love and support of our loyal customers. We've maintained our commitment to quality while expanding our menu to include innovative chai variations and traditional Indian snacks.

Our Promise

Today, Chai-Wala stands as a testament to the timeless appeal of authentic Indian chai. We continue to serve each cup with the same passion and dedication that marked our beginnings, ensuring that every visitor experiences the warmth and comfort of true Indian hospitality.
